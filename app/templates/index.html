No pressure.

Opt in, share when you're free, and pick what you're up for - coffee, a walk, eating out, or a small-group meal. Tuck Meet quietly finds overlaps and emails a friendly introduction. No chat. No profiles. No feed. No tracking.

How it works

  1. Sign up with your @tuck.dartmouth.edu email and verify it.
  2. Choose your availability and the kinds of meetups you're open to.
  3. Each day at 5 PM we look for overlaps and send low-key intros.
  4. Meet if it works - skip it if you're busy. Opt out any time.
